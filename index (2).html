Finn The Human Boy

Attack: 8 | Defense: 6

Special Move: Righteous fury doubles attack when defending a friend

description

Finn is a brave and adventurous boy with blonde hair, wearing a white bear hat and a blue shirt. He’s always ready to help others, using his sword to fight monsters and protect his friends. Finn is loyal, kind, and always seeks to do what’s right, even if it’s tough. He shares his adventures with his best friend, Jake the dog.

Jake The Dog

Attack: 7 | Defense: 7

Special Move: Mega morph change shape to block or bypass any obstacle once per battle.

description

Jake is a magical, talking dog with the ability to stretch and shape-shift. He’s Finn’s best friend and has a relaxed, easygoing personality. Jake loves having fun, but he’s also very wise and often gives Finn advice. He’s loyal, playful, and always ready to help out in any adventure, using his stretchy powers to get out of tricky situations. He cares deeply for Finn and has a big heart.

BMO

Attack: 5 | Defense: 4

Special Move: Digital Daydream Distracts opponents with game and charm,lowering their next attack

description

BMO is a small, playful robot with a big personality. They look like a mix between a game console and a VHS player, with a screen that shows emotions. BMO is fun, imaginative, and loves playing games, singing songs, and making jokes. Despite being small, BMO is very loyal to their friends and always ready to help out. They bring a lot of energy and cheer to any adventure!
